    Discover the outdoors in style, or just add some outdoors style to your everyday outfit, with New Balance's All Terrain range.

    The All Terrain Pullover Hoodie in Vintage Teal is designed to keep you comfy on those more rugged adventures whilst being an ideal warm layer on those closer to home adventures. Made from 100% cotton with a plush brushed back fleece inner and a regular fit comfort is guaranteed.

    Ribbed cuffs and hem secure the fit while a drawcord adjustable hood adds extra cover. Of course no pullover Hoodie is complete without a kangaroo pouch pocket.

    New Balance's All Terrain patch sit on the chest with the All Terrain script screen printed down the sleeve.
